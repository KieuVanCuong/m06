AI Features and Third-Party Processing

When you use an AI feature in AI Note Pro Docs (summarize, auto-tag, improve writing, translate, or AI chat), the text you choose to process — including the titles and content of the notes involved and the messages you send in AI chat — is transmitted, over an encrypted connection, to our processing service and then to Google's Gemini API so it can generate a result.

  • What we collect and send: the note/journal text and chat messages you actively submit to an AI feature. This data is sent only when you tap an AI action — never automatically or in the background.
  • How it is collected: directly from the content you enter in the app.
  • How it is used: solely to generate the requested AI result (summary, tags, rewritten text, translation, or chat reply). We do not use it for advertising and we do not sell it.
  • Who receives it: our processing backend and Google (Gemini API). Google processes the request under its own terms; see Google's privacy documentation for the Gemini API. Google provides protections for this data that are comparable to those described in this policy.
  • Storage: your notes are stored locally on your device. We do not retain a copy of your notes on our servers beyond what is needed to process a request.
  • Your control: AI features are optional. You are asked to consent before the first time any data is sent, and you can enable or disable AI data sharing at any time in Settings → AI & Data Sharing. If you decline, all note-taking features continue to work.
